Freedom in Christ
Foundation Module — Neil Anderson Framework
Foundation · Core Identity
You Are Free in Christ
"It is for freedom that Christ has set us free. Stand firm, then, and do not let yourselves be burdened again by a yoke of slavery." — Galatians 5:1
🔑 What You Must Know

You are not defined by your past. You are not defeated by your struggles. You are not abandoned by God. Your identity is completely rooted in Christ, not in your sexuality, your wounds, or your failures.

Your Identity in Christ (36 Statements):

In Christ, you are...

  • Accepted: You are God's child (John 1:12)
  • Chosen: You are chosen and blameless (Ephesians 1:4)
  • Redeemed: Your sins are forgiven (Ephesians 1:7)
  • Righteous: You are the righteousness of God (2 Cor 5:21)
  • Holy: You are sanctified and set apart (1 Cor 1:2)
  • Sealed: The Holy Spirit marks you as His (Ephesians 1:13)
  • Alive: Alive to God and dead to sin (Romans 6:11)
  • Complete: You lack nothing in Christ (Colossians 2:10)

These are not feelings. These are legal facts based on Christ's work. Your emotions will catch up as you believe them.

The Lies You've Believed:

The enemy has deceived you with lies. Satan is "the father of lies" (John 8:44) and specializes in:

  • ❌ "You are your past"
  • ❌ "God can't forgive what you've done"
  • ❌ "You are too dirty for God"
  • ❌ "Your desires define you"
  • ❌ "You are unlovable"
  • ❌ "This will never change"
  • ❌ "God abandoned you"

These are lies. They contradict Scripture. Your job is to reject them and replace them with truth.

The Process (Freedom in Christ Steps):
1
Renounce lies you've believed about yourself
2
Renounce false agreements with the enemy
3
Declare the truth of your identity in Christ
4
Choose to believe what God says over how you feel
5
Renew your mind by meditating on Scripture daily
Declaration of Truth
"I am free from condemnation. I am accepted in Christ. My past does not define me. My sins are forgiven and forgotten. I am righteous through Jesus. I reject the lies of the enemy. I declare myself alive to God and dead to sin. I am not what happened to me. I am who Christ says I am."
📖 Foundation Scripture: Romans 8:1 — "There is now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus."
⚡ Key Understanding: You don't need to feel free to be free. Freedom is a legal position based on Christ's work, not a feeling. Your emotions will follow as you renew your mind with truth.
📝 Exercise

Identify the lies: What has the enemy told you about your identity? Write down 3-5 lies you've believed. Now, for each one, find a Scripture that contradicts it. Declare the truth out loud. This breaks the power of the lie.
