Soul ties are spiritual connections that bind you to others. Not all are healthy. Some open doors to demonic influence.
⚠️ What Are Soul Ties?

A soul tie is a spiritual bond between two people. They form through:

  • Sexual relations — "the two shall become one flesh" (1 Cor 6:16)
  • Deep emotional connection — shared trauma, dependency
  • Spiritual alignment — agreement with someone's beliefs or practices
  • Demonic agreement — unholy vows, curses, false covenants

Healthy soul ties exist: Marriage, family, mentors, healthy friendships.
Unhealthy ties must be broken: Fornication, abuse, occult practices, false agreements.

🔥 Sexual Soul Ties
When you join your body with someone sexually outside of marriage, you create a "one flesh" bond. This includes:
  • Fornication (sex before/outside marriage)
  • Pornography (joining your body to an image)
  • Same-sex relations (unholy covenant)
  • Masturbation while imagining someone

Scripture: "Do you not know that he who unites himself with a prostitute is one with her in body?" (1 Cor 6:16)

Effects: Ongoing emotional connection to that person, intrusive thoughts, inability to forget them, spiritual stronghold in your mind and emotions, demonic access.
Sexual Tie Breaking Declaration
"In the name of Jesus Christ, I sever and break every sexual soul tie with [person's name]. I declare that my body, my sexuality, and my emotions are not joined to them anymore. I reclaim my body as a temple of the Holy Spirit. I am no longer one flesh with them. I close the door I opened through sexual sin. By the blood of Jesus, all demonic claims through this tie are cancelled. I am free."
💔 Emotional Soul Ties
Unhealthy emotional bonds that create dependency, usually involving:
  • Obsessive thinking about someone
  • Emotional dependency or co-dependency
  • Shared trauma bonding
  • Idolizing another person
  • Fear of abandonment

These can form even without sexual involvement and open doors to manipulation and control.

Signs You Have Emotional Ties: Thinking about them constantly, unable to move on, your mood depends on their mood, you'll compromise your values for them, you can't make decisions without their input.
Emotional Tie Breaking Declaration
"I break the emotional soul tie with [person/people]. I renounce my unhealthy dependency on them. I take back my emotions from them. I am whole in Christ alone, not in any person. I sever the emotional cord that binds me to their moods, their approval, or their presence. I declare my emotional health belongs to God."
🙏 Spiritual Soul Ties
Agreements with someone's spiritual beliefs or practices:
  • Occult practices or New Age teachings
  • False religions or cults
  • Demonic music, literature, or media
  • Agreements with false prophets
  • Involvement in witchcraft or sorcery

These ties open specific demonic doors and give the enemy legal access to your life.

⚡ Critical: If you've been involved in occult practices, you must specifically renounce them and break these ties, or demons retain legal ground.
Spiritual Tie Breaking Declaration
"I renounce and break every spiritual tie formed through [occultic practice/false belief/demonic source]. I renounce the lies I believed. I break agreement with all demonic forces attached to this practice. I declare my allegiance is to Jesus Christ alone. I belong to Him. All demonic legal ground through this tie is cancelled by His blood."
👹 Demonic Soul Ties
Direct demonic covenants formed through:
  • Explicit vows made to the enemy
  • Participation in satanic rituals
  • Curses spoken over you (generational or direct)
  • Trauma designed to create demonic entry
  • False covenants or soul transfers

These are the most serious and must be aggressively broken through the name and blood of Jesus.

⚠️ Warfare Alert: When breaking demonic ties, you may experience increased demonic activity briefly as demons lose their legal ground. This is normal. Stand firm in Christ's authority.
Demonic Tie Breaking Declaration
"In the name and by the blood of Jesus Christ, I break and sever every demonic soul tie. I renounce every vow, curse, agreement, or covenant made with demons. I take back all legal ground they claimed through these ties. I declare that the blood of Jesus Christ cleanses me completely. All demonic claims are cancelled. I am redeemed, restored, and set free. Demons have NO authority over me. I belong to Jesus Christ."
If you were trafficked or abused: These declarations are powerful, but consider working with a Christian deliverance minister. Some cases require additional spiritual authority. Reach out to your church.
